The Filipino Stew:
Chicken Adobo

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 45 mins
Total Time: 1 hr 5 mins
Servings: 6

D e s c r i p t i o n

Adobo, a vinegar-based braise, is the Philippines's national dish. Chicken pieces are marinated in soy sauce and spices, pan-fried, and stewed until tender. This marinade also includes other herbs and flavorings like garlic, bay leaves, and whole peppercorns.

I n g r e d i e n t s

  • 2 table spoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

S t e p s

  1. Heat vegatable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken pieaces 2-3 minutes per side and then trasnfer that chicken to a plate to set aside.
  2. add onion and garlic to the skillet; cook until softened and brown (6 minutes)
  3. Pour soy sauce and vinegar, season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf
  4. Return chicken to pan on high heat, bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to a medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through (35-40 minutes)
